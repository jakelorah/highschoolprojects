Objectives and Requirements for Building a Website for Myself and my Family to Access


Objective:
The objective of the project is for my family to have easy access to pictures, calendars, events, and contact info on the web whenever they'd like.

Requirements:
1) There must be a side bar on the left side of the page with links to different pages. Those pages are pictures, calendars, events, and contact info.

2) The color scheme of the website must be easy to read and not to crazy. I recommend all light colors.

3) The website must be private. Only certain people can access it by a code.

4) There must be music playing in the backround when you are on the website. Nice soothing music.

5) The pictures page must have a next and a back button when you go through the pictures.

6) There must be a comment page on the bottom of the page for feedback and suggestions.

7) The calendar page should have events and birthdays in the calendar and the events page should just be a list of all of the events.

8) The font should be either times new roman or comic sans for everything except the title of the page. That should be popping out. That font is up to you.

9) Contact info page should have name, phone number, email and address.

10) Pictures, calendars, events, and contact info pages must be easy to change around and add additional information to them. I recommend PDF files for those pages.
