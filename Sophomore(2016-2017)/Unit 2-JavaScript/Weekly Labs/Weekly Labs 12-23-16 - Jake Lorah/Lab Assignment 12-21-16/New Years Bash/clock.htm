FrostiRun Kid's Korner Chester the Jester A Taste of Tulsa Winter Art Fair Lester's Blues Band The Jazz Express The Fireworks Show Final Countdown

Tulsa's New Year's Bash is back for the 22nd year of fun and excitement. The festivities start on December 31 at noon with the FrostiRun 5K and 10K races. Sign up now or the morning of the race. Family fun is available at the Kid's Korner with booths and free activities for the whole family; including performances from Tulsa's own Chester the Jester. The fun continues with A Taste of Tulsa as vendors provide samples from Tulsa's best eateries and for the artistically inclined, plan to attend the Winter Art Fair on Tulsa's downtown square.

Music from Lester's Blues Band and The Jazz Express brings us into the New Year. The dancing starts at 7 p.m. and continues until midnight. From 10 to 11 p.m. we say goodbye to the old year (and ring in the new) with the famous New Year's Bash fireworks spectacular. Be sure to arrive early for all of the festivities and the final countdown.
